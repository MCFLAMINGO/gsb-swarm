## Standard Email Template

```
Subject: Public Records Request — Active Business Tax Receipt Data, [COUNTY] County

To Whom It May Concern:

Pursuant to Florida Public Records Law, § 119.07(1), F.S., I am requesting the following public records:

A current export of all active Business Tax Receipts (local business licenses) for [COUNTY] County, in CSV or Excel format, including the following fields where available:

- Business name
- Business address (street, city, ZIP code)
- Business category or NAICS code
- License/receipt type
- Issue date and expiration date
- Account or receipt number

This data will be used for municipal economic intelligence research. No personally identifiable information (SSN, personal addresses) is requested.

Please acknowledge receipt of this request within 5 business days as required by Florida law.
If there is a fee for this request, please notify me before fulfilling.
```
